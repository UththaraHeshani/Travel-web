Galle Is A Historic Coastal City Located In The Southwestern Part Of Sri Lanka. It Is The Administrative Capital Of The Galle District And Is Known For Its Rich History, Cultural Heritage, And Scenic Beauty. The City Is Situated On The Southwestern Tip Of The Island And Is Surrounded By The Indian Ocean On Three Sides. Galle Has A Fascinating History That Dates Back Centuries. It Was A Major Port City And An Important Trading Hub During The Colonial Era. The City Has Been Influenced By Various Civilizations, Including The Portuguese, Dutch, And British, Which Is Evident In Its Architecture, Fortifications, And Cultural Traditions.
