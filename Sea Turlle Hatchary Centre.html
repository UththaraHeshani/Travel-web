Sea Turlle Hatchary Centre

There is a Sea Turtle Hatchery Center located in Mahamodara, Galle, Sri Lanka. The center is known as the "Sea Turtle Hatchery and Rescue Center Mahamodara." It is a popular attraction that focuses on sea turtle conservation efforts and provides a sanctuary for injured and endangered sea turtles.

At the Sea Turtle Hatchery and Rescue Center Mahamodara, you can learn about the life cycle of sea turtles, the threats they face, and the conservation efforts being undertaken to protect them. The center works to rescue injured sea turtles, care for them, and release them back into the ocean once they have recovered. They also aim to protect sea turtle nests, ensuring that the hatchlings have a safe journey to the sea.Visitors to the hatchery can observe various species of sea turtles, including green turtles, hawksbill turtles, and loggerhead turtles. You may have the opportunity to see baby turtles in the hatchery tanks or witness the release of the hatchlings into the ocean.

The Sea Turtle Hatchery and Rescue Center Mahamodara is located in the Mahamodara area of Galle, which is approximately 2 kilometers (1.2 miles) east of the Galle city center. It is easily accessible by taxi or tuk-tuk.Please note that operational hours and visiting policies may vary, so it's advisable to check the center's opening hours and any entry requirements before your visit. Additionally, it's always important to be respectful of the sea turtles and follow any guidelines provided by the center to ensure their well-being.
