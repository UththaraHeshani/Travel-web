Duwili Ella

Neluwa Duwili Ella, also known as Kosmulla Dowili Ella, is indeed a waterfall located in the Neluwa area, which is near Galle in Sri Lanka.Duwili Ella is situated in the village of Kosmulla, and it is one of the lesser-known waterfalls in the region. It is a relatively smaller waterfall but offers a beautiful natural setting surrounded by lush greenery and rocks.

To reach Neluwa Duwili Ella, you would need to travel on the route towards Lankagama from Neluwa. It is advisable to hire a local guide or obtain detailed directions from locals or tourist information centers to ensure you can find the waterfall easily, as it may not be well-marked or have prominent signage.

Please note that conditions, accessibility, and safety precautions for visiting waterfalls can change over time. It's always a good idea to check the current status and obtain updated information before planning a visit to Neluwa Duwili Ella or any other waterfall in the area.
