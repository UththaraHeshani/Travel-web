Koggla Beach

Koggala Beach is a beautiful coastal area located near the town of Koggala, which is approximately 20 kilometers (12 miles) east of Galle.Koggala Beach offers a long stretch of golden sand and clear blue waters, making it an appealing destination for beach lovers. The beach is generally less crowded compared to some of the more popular tourist beaches in Sri Lanka, providing a more serene and tranquil atmosphere.

Visitors can enjoy various activities at Koggala Beach, including swimming, sunbathing, and leisurely walks along the shoreline. The beach also serves as a starting point for boat rides and tours to explore the nearby Koggala Lake, which is known for its scenic beauty and biodiversity.Koggala Beach is not only known for its natural beauty but also for the Koggala Beach Rock, which is a distinctive rock formation situated just offshore. It adds to the picturesque scenery and is often a subject of fascination for photographers.

Additionally, Koggala is home to the Martin Wickramasinghe Folk Museum Complex, dedicated to the famous Sri Lankan author, Martin Wickramasinghe. The museum exhibits various artifacts and displays that provide insight into the traditional Sri Lankan village life and culture.To reach Koggala Beach, you can take a short drive or hire a tuk-tuk from Galle. The beach is easily accessible and can be reached within 30 minutes by road.Koggala Beach offers a more laid-back and less crowded beach experience, making it an attractive option for those seeking relaxation and natural beauty.
